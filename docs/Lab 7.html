The Straw Hat Pirates

Monkey D. Luffy

Monkey D Luffy was born in a small village on an island in the East Blue, one of the Four Blues or seas. He was raised by his Grandpa Garp, a legendary Marine, who eventually left him in the care of Dadan, a reformed mountain bandit. His childhood friends were his adoptive brothers Portgas D. Ace and Sabo, and the legendary pirate Red Haired Shanks. Shanks was celebrating in the local bar with Luffy and his crew after recovering one of the mysterious Devil Fruit when Luffy ate it while their back was turned. The Devil Fruit turned out to be the Gum-Gum Fruit and it turned Luffy into a Rubber Man. When he turned 16 Luffy set out to become King of the Pirates, a title once held by the most free man in the world, Gol D. Roger. Now he just needs to gather a crew.

Roronoa Zoro

Roronoa Zoro was born and raised as an orphan abandoned at the dojo of a great swordsman. He trained his entire life and eventually developed a rivalry with the sword-masters daughter, Kuina. When she died due to a tragic accident Zoro vowed to become the worlds greatest swordsman. He met Luffy and joined the crew after a run in with Axe Hand Morgan, a corrupt Marine general. Later after a run in with Hawkeye Mihawk, currently the worlds strongest swordsman, Zoro swore to Lufy that he would never lose again and has since been First Mate.

Nami

Nami was born in an unfortunate town in the East Blue which happened to get raided by pirates. Nami and her sister Nojiko survived the attack and were found by her adoptive mother Bell-mere. They're home later got taken hostage by Arlong, who killed Bell-mere in the process, and Nami would be forced to act as their navigator and rob pirates in order save up enough to buy her home. She met Luffy and joined the crew after robbing them and being chased back to Arlong Park where Luffy defeated Arlong convincing Nami to join.

Usopp

Usopp was born in Syrup village on an island in the East Blue. He was raised by his mother after his father Yasopp left to join the Red Haired Pirates. Usopp spent his childhood hearing stories from his mother about how his father was a great warrior of the sea, until she passed due to illness. Usopp later befriended a sick girl who lived outside the village and cheered he up with his fake stories of adventure. He met Luffy and Joined the crew when his village was attacked by Captain Kuro who Luffy defeated. Usopp would later gain the title of "God" due to a misunderstanding at the Dressrosa incedent.

Vinsmoke Sanji

Sanji was born to the Vinsmoke family, leaders of the Germa 66. He was tormented and abused as a child by both his father and his three brothers Ichi, Niji, and Yanji. He fell overboard when the cruise ship he was on with his family was attacked by pirates before a storm hit washing Sanji and the pirate captain overboard. The captain was legendary pirate Red Foot Zeff, he and Sanji were stranded for weeks before rescue. After this incident Zeff established the Baratie a sea restaurant and trained Sanji to be a chef. He joined the crew a the chef when Luffy defeated Don Krieg who was attacking the Baratie.

Tony Tony Chopper

Tony Tony Chopper was born a blue nosed reindeer on Drum Island in the Grand Line. All of the other reindeer used to laugh and call him names, then one foggy evening he stumbled upon the Human-Human Devil Fruit taking a bite and making him a human reindeer. He was then outcast from his herd and eventually found himself in the care of Dr. Hiriluk who shared his dream of healing all the worlds illnesses with Chopper. Hiriluk passed several months later due to a disease he had been hiding. Chopper met Luffy and joined the crew when he came to Drum Island and defeated Wapol returning the island to Dalton.

Nico Robin

Nico Robin was born and raised on the Island of Ohara, the home of the Ohara Scholars. She desperately wanted to be a Scholar like her mother who went out adventure while she was young. She was eventually allowed into the library where she learned that the Scholars could read the ancient language of the Poneglyphs which had been outlawed by the World Government. When they caught wind that the Scholars could decipher the Poneglyphs the World Government destroyed Ohara with only Robin escaping. The government labeled her a devil due to her knowledge in deciphering the ancient text and ability to uncover the Void Century. She met up with Luffy while she was allied with Crocodile, one of the Seven Warlords of the Sea, who was trying take over the kingdom of Alabasta before being defeated by Luffy.
